Our solution

Our project leveraged a robust tech stack to achieve high-performance event retrieval. We employed CLIP for multimodal embeddings, enabling seamless integration of visual and textual information. To enhance efficiency, we extracted keyframes from videos using TransNetv2 and KMeans clustering. This streamlined the retrieval process, allowing our model to focus on the most relevant segments.

  • Key-frame Extraction TransNetV2, K-Means.
  • OCR Vietname-OCR.
  • Multi-modal Embedding CLIP
  • Translation Google Translation, PyTranslator, Transformers-based models, etc.
  • LLM-based Approach Query Refinement, etc.
